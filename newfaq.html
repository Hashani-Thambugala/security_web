Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about forensic investigations, confidentiality, process, and collaboration with Sherlock-X.

What makes Sherlock-X different from other investigation firms?

Sherlock-X combines classic detective methodology with advanced AI-driven forensics, global field agents, and military-grade encryption. We handle each case with absolute discretion, and our interdisciplinary team includes cyber experts, former intelligence analysts, and surveillance specialists. Over 98% case success rate and zero confidentiality breach in 20 years.

Do you operate internationally?

Yes. With partner offices in North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and the Middle East, we conduct cross-border investigations, asset tracing, and digital evidence retrieval in over 45 countries. Our network ensures rapid on-ground intelligence wherever needed.

How does the investigation lifecycle work?

We follow a 5‑stage protocol: (1) Confidential consultation & risk assessment, (2) Tailored strategy & forensic planning, (3) Covert data acquisition & field surveillance, (4) Evidence analysis & chain-of-custody, (5) Final intelligence report with legal advisory. Updates provided through secure channels.

How long does an investigation take?

Timeline varies based on complexity. Basic background checks may take 3-5 business days; complex corporate espionage or digital forensic cases average 2-6 weeks. We provide realistic milestones and expedited options for urgent matters.

Is my identity protected?

Absolutely. All client data is protected under NDA and GDPR/CCPA standards. We use end-to-end encrypted portals, anonymous case coding, and never share case details without explicit consent. Our reputation is built on discretion.

Do you sign non-disclosure agreements?

Yes, NDAs are mandatory for every engagement. We provide a mutual confidentiality agreement before any case details are shared. You retain full ownership over all evidence and final reports.

What technology does Sherlock-X use for digital forensics?

Our cyber lab utilizes proprietary AI pattern recognition, blockchain timestamping for evidence integrity, dark web crawlers, and advanced mobile forensics (Cellebrite, Axiom). We also conduct penetration testing and data recovery from encrypted drives.

Can you assist with corporate pre-employment screening?

Yes. We offer executive-level background verification, financial integrity checks, credential validation, and conflict of interest mapping – all compliant with local labor laws and privacy regulations.

How is pricing structured?

We offer transparent fixed-fee packages for standard services (background checks, asset tracing). For customized investigations, we provide a detailed quote after initial consultation. No hidden costs, and billing is itemized.

Do I need to be a corporation to hire Sherlock-X?

Not at all. We serve private individuals, legal firms, high-net-worth families, and corporations alike. Matters range from infidelity & missing persons to fraud and intellectual property theft.

Still have unanswered questions?

Our intelligence liaison team is ready to provide confidential assistance and personalized answers.
